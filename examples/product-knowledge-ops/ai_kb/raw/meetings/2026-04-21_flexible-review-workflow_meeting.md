# Flexible Review Workflow Planning Meeting

The team discussed a flexible review workflow that lets users start a review request and choose one of
several participating review paths at review routing time.

Confirmed notes:

- Flexible Review Workflow depends on the Core Knowledge Portal review intake and evidence ledger.
- Knowledge AI Assistant may recommend review options based on review type and knowledge area.
- Team onboarding needs a reusable "review configuration" capability shared with Team Workflow Expansion.
- The current launch target is a small beta with anonymized reviewer groups.

Open questions:

- Whether unused review choices expire independently or with the parent workflow.
- Whether AI recommendations should be visible before submission or only during review routing.
